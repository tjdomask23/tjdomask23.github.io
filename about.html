A bit more

I'm a UW-Madison CS grad working as a Business Systems Analyst at Rockwell Automation, in the Milwaukee area. The job is mostly about taking systems that grew up tangled — too many tools, too many ways of doing the same thing — and giving teams one standard that actually sticks. I like that work more than the title makes it sound.

The throughline in everything I do is systems design. At Rockwell it's Jira standards and process waste. On my own time it's a fitness app built on actual research, or a tabletop RPG ruleset I designed from nothing and run for five friends. Same instinct in both: build something structured enough to be reliable and flexible enough that people enjoy using it.

Away from a keyboard I'm a competitive-swimmer-turned-rec-leaguer — volleyball and softball these days. I keep a music library that's gotten genuinely out of hand, watch more anime than I'll admit in a professional bio, and spend real time on the worldbuilding side of tabletop games. And I'm a dog person, no qualifiers.

If any of that lines up with something you're working on — or you just want to compare campaign notes — reach out.
